Information Technology

Information Technology (ITEC) is the multidisciplinary study, design, development, implementation, support or management of computer-based information systems.

At GGC, we offer Bachelor of Science degree in information technology.

Potential Job Positions

Information Technology program prepares students for the following job positions after graduation:
  • Software/App developer
  • Website developer
  • Gae developer
  • Database administrator
  • Network and security administrator
  • Data analyst
  • And many more...
